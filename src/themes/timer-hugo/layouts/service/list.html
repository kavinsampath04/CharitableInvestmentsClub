One of the big things we offer is a course on investing. Written by the founder, Kavin Sampath, you can complete the entire course with no prior knowledge in only 2 hours. The course itself covers everything from the basics in how the market itself works to the ways you can analyze and buy stocks.




Our club itself uses a detailed strategy to buy stocks that we call the "Bullseye" Strategy. The bullseye strategy uses ten factors (5 quantitative and 5 qualitative) to evaluate how good a stock will perform. Each factor is assigned a value from 1-5 by our team of analysts. The lower the value, the better because it brings a stock closer to the bullseye.
